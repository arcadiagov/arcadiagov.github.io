Government of Arcadia — Official News Release

Lennox Steeves Admits Espionage Role for Lily Empire, Remains Minister of Online Security

May 21, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

In a shocking development, Lennox Steeves, the current Minister of Online Security of Bradictoria, publicly admitted that his official position has been used as a cover for spying on Emperor Bradley Christie on behalf of the Lily Empire.

Despite this revelation, Steeves remains in his ministerial post, raising serious concerns about internal security and governance within Bradictoria’s administration.

The Government of Arcadia condemns this breach of trust and espionage, calling for immediate accountability and review of Bradictoria's security protocols. Arcadia remains vigilant and committed to protecting regional stability and transparency.

Investigations are ongoing to uncover the full scope of this intelligence operation and any further involvement by other officials or foreign entities.
