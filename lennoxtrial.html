Charges Filed

  • Imperial Treason (T01) — Attempted subversion of Arcadian sovereignty and betrayal of allegiance to the Gheetan Empire.
  • Cyber Espionage (C04) — Unauthorized infiltration of government systems and breach of classified operations.
  • Terrorism (V15) — Acts causing destabilization, widespread panic, and endangerment of civilian life.
  • Aiding and Abetting Imperial Treason (T02) — Applicable to any associates aiding in his evasion of justice.

Arrest Warrant Summary

The fugitive Lennox Steeves is wanted for a series of grievous offenses against the Kingdom of Arcadia and its allied sovereign, the Gheetan Empire. He has been linked to multiple high-impact cyber attacks that have disrupted state infrastructure and compromised national security. His actions represent an existential threat to the stability and integrity of both realms.

Legal Note

This warrant was officially issued on May 2, 2025 at 20:34 hours (8:34 PM). It remains in full effect until the accused is apprehended and brought before the joint Arcadian–Gheetan tribunal for prosecution. Any citizen found assisting Steeves may be prosecuted under Clause T02.
