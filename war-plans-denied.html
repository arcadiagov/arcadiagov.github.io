Government of Arcadia — Official News Release

Bradictoria Denies All Leaked War Plans

May 21, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

The Emperor of Bradictoria has personally addressed the rumors regarding leaked war plans, firmly denying any truth to the allegations.

In a public statement, the Emperor said:

“There are no war plans. These baseless leaks are nothing more than attempts to spread falsehoods and create unrest. Bradictoria remains committed to peace and diplomatic dialogue with all neighbors.”

The Emperor further emphasized that the government is actively pursuing those responsible for spreading misinformation and reaffirmed the nation’s dedication to regional stability.

Citizens and international partners alike are urged to remain cautious of unverified reports and support efforts to maintain trust and cooperation.
