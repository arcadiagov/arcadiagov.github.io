Charges Filed

  • High Treason (T01) — Willful betrayal of the Kingdom Realm of Arcadia through the orchestration of illegitimate power structures and attempts to undermine sovereign rule.
  • Usurpation of Foreign Authority (P17) — Unauthorized assumption of power over a territory outside the legal jurisdiction of the accused.
  • Unauthorized Assumption of Title (P18) — Illegally adopting the title of “Emperor” without constitutional recognition or legal appointment.
  • Sedition and Destabilization of State Order (S05) — Promoting disorder, disunity, and rebellion against the Arcadian state apparatus.
  • Attempted Coercion of the Lordly Government (R03) — Applying unlawful pressure and manipulation toward the High King and his ministers.
  • Impersonation of Government Office (F08) — Falsely presenting oneself as a legally authorized agent of foreign or domestic administration.
  • Fabrication of a Foreign State Entity (S09) — Illegally establishing or declaring a non-recognized political entity in defiance of diplomatic and territorial protocols.
  • Symbolic Defilement of Arcadian Honor (R07) — Acts or declarations that insult, undermine, or degrade the symbols, titles, and sovereign dignity of Arcadia.
  • Aiding and Abetting High Treason (T02) — Applicable to any persons providing assistance, shelter, or material support to the fugitive Gavin Jones.

Arrest Warrant Summary

The fugitive Gavin Jones is hereby declared an enemy of the Kingdom Realm of Arcadia, having engaged in acts of treason, sedition, and sovereign impersonation. His unlawful declaration of the “Gavonion Isles” and self-proclamation as Emperor constitute grave offenses against international order and the legitimacy of established governance. These charges have been reviewed and updated as of the most recent intelligence received from loyalist outposts.

Legal Note

This warrant was officially updated on May 27, 2025 at 18:10 hours (6:10 PM). It remains in full effect until the accused is apprehended and brought before the Arcadian Tribunal for formal prosecution. Any individual aiding the accused shall be considered complicit under Article T02 and face equal prosecution under the Arcadian Penal Code.
