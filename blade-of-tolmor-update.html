Government of Arcadia — Official News Release

Royal Blade Renamed High Blade of Tolmor

May 20, 2025 – High Court of Arcadia

In a decree issued this morning, His Lordship the Lord High King of Arcadia has formally renamed the ceremonial Royal Blade to the High Blade of Tolmor.

While its ceremonial role remains unchanged, the High Blade of Tolmor will take on heightened symbolic prominence during coronations, state rituals, and declarations of royal will.

The renaming pays homage to Tolmor, the fabled Arcadian warrior-saint whose valor and sacrifice are etched into the soul of the kingdom. The High Blade of Tolmor is now recognized as a living symbol of Arcadian sovereignty, justice, and ancestral strength.

Heraldic authorities have begun the process of updating all royal insignia, flags, and seals to reflect the new designation. Citizens and officials alike have praised the change as both poetic and powerful.
