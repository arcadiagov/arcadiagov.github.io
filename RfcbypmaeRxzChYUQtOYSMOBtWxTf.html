Status: Deceased — Former Emperor of the Flake Empire
Documents Affiliated: Flake Empire Charter, Assassination Report
Information: Isaac Bell was the self-declared Emperor of the Flake Empire, a micronation he attempted to establish. The Flake Empire lasted for only one month before its sudden collapse due to internal instability and external pressure. Bell was assassinated under mysterious circumstances shortly after the dissolution of the Flake Empire, which was considered a failed attempt at sovereignty. His efforts to create a new entity were met with widespread resistance from both local authorities and international powers.
Concerns: Although the Flake Empire was short-lived, Bell's assassination remains under investigation. There are ongoing concerns about potential factions that supported his empire or benefited from its brief existence, which may still be in play. Surveillance of his former associates and any ongoing activities connected to the Flake Empire is advised.

Dexter Welling

Status: Former High King of Arcadia, Impeached
Documents Affiliated: Impeachment Documents, Royal Diplomatic Records
Information: Dexter Welling served as the first High King of Arcadia. However, his reign ended abruptly when he was impeached after committing **Grand Larceny** and destroying crucial documents bearing the Royal Seal and Signature. These actions were deemed a direct violation of Arcadian law, undermining both the monarchy and national security. Before his impeachment, Welling held a diplomatic position, representing Arcadia in various international matters. However, following his removal from office, he is no longer affiliated with Arcadia.
Concerns: Dexter Welling's impeachment has left a permanent stain on Arcadian history. While no longer in power or affiliated, his prior actions have raised concerns about internal corruption within the Arcadian government. Surveillance and caution are advised with regards to any of his former diplomatic associates or potential factions that may seek to restore his influence.

Logan Boucher

Status: Fugitive — Disgraced Former Head of State
Documents Affiliated: Royal Discharge Decree, Arrest Warrant, Diplomatic Incident Reports
Information: Logan Boucher held office as Head of State of Arcadia during a volatile period in the Kingdom’s history. His brief tenure was marked by reckless decision-making and unauthorized threats of war against both the Great Gheetan Empire and the Republic of Bradectoria. These actions violated multiple provisions of his sworn oath to the High King and irreparably damaged Arcadia’s diplomatic posture.

Following a formal Royal Discharge and unanimous High Council condemnation, Boucher was stripped of his office and a warrant was issued for his arrest. He remains at large.
Concerns: Boucher’s inflammatory rhetoric and defiance of royal authority severely undermined Arcadian foreign policy and national credibility. While no longer considered a high-level threat due to his loss of influence and support, the Kingdom maintains an active search protocol. Surveillance is recommended, particularly for political groups sympathetic to his past actions.
