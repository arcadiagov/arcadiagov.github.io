Government of Arcadia — Official News Release

The Legend of Tolmor

May 20, 2025 – Department of Cultural Heritage

Tolmor, the mythic warrior-saint of Arcadian lore, is one of the kingdom's most revered and enigmatic figures. Though no definitive records confirm his existence, the legend of Tolmor has been passed down through generations as a symbol of courage, sacrifice, and divine protection.

While scholars debate his historicity, the cultural impact of Tolmor is undeniable. His name is invoked in rituals, military ceremonies, and literature. To honor this enduring legend, the Royal Blade has been officially renamed the High Blade of Tolmor, ensuring his spirit remains etched in the legacy of Arcadian sovereignty.

Whether man or myth, Tolmor embodies the virtues that Arcadia holds dear: loyalty, valor, and eternal vigilance against tyranny.
