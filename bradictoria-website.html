Bradictoria Launches Its Official Website

May 3, 2025 — The territory of Bradictoria has launched its official website, presenting itself with a formal online presence for the first time.

The site includes pages outlining governmental structures, leadership claims, and territorial assertions. The move signals Bradictoria's intention to maintain a digital platform for its communications and public-facing information.

While Arcadian authorities have not recognized Bradictoria’s sovereignty, the launch marks a notable development in the region's political dynamics.

The site is available at: bradictoria.github.io/index.html
