Binding Pact Between the Lord High King of Arcadia and Bradley Christie

May 20, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

In a historic agreement, the Lord High King of Arcadia and Bradley Christie have signed a binding pact pledging that neither nation will attempt to hack or infiltrate the other's official websites.

Both leaders further vowed to use all means within their power to prevent any officials or agents under their command from engaging in such cyber activities, ensuring mutual respect for digital sovereignty.

This pact marks a significant step toward fostering trust and cooperation between Arcadia and Bradictoria in the realm of cybersecurity and peaceful relations.
