Overview

The Kingdom Realm of Arcadia is a sovereign micronation dedicated to cultural enrichment, self-governance, and community development. Founded upon a strong constitutional foundation, Arcadia is ruled by the Lord High King and guided by a High Council and Head of State. Citizens of Arcadia enjoy full civil rights and participate in the shaping of its future.

Government

Arcadia is governed by a constitutional monarchy led by the Lord High King. The government is structured into executive, legislative, and judicial branches. The High King exercises executive authority while the High Council and Head of State assist in legislative processes and governance. Recent acts and laws are passed to ensure a stable and just society.

Union with the Gheetan Empire

As of 2025, Arcadia has entered into a Treaty of Eternal Union with the Great Gheetan Empire. This alliance affirms mutual cooperation, defense, and shared governance principles while maintaining Arcadia's unique autonomy and national identity. The government continues to align its legal framework with Gheetan standards while preserving its distinct traditions and structures.

Culture and Identity

Arcadia takes pride in its rich heritage, regal traditions, and dedication to progress. Citizens celebrate national holidays, participate in local governance, and engage in public discourse. From classical education initiatives to cultural arts, Arcadia nurtures a deeply rooted national identity.

Citizenship

Citizenship in Arcadia is a privilege extended to those who uphold its values and participate in its civic life. Individuals may apply for citizenship through formal channels and are granted rights and responsibilities upon approval. Apply for Citizenship.
