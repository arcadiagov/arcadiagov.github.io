Official Diplomatic Protest Sent to Bradictorian Emperor

May 15, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

The Kingdom of Arcadia has formally dispatched an official diplomatic protest letter to His Imperial Majesty Emperor Bradley Christie of the Bradictorian Empire. This action follows the recent appointment of Lennox Steeves — a convicted cyber espionage criminal — to the position of Minister of Online Security within the Bradictorian government.

Arcadian officials have demanded the immediate dismissal of Mr. Steeves, emphasizing that such a decision undermines mutual trust and the longstanding alliance between the two nations. The letter underscores that failure to remove the appointee may force Arcadia to reconsider the terms of bilateral cooperation.

The Lord High King’s office confirmed the letter’s dispatch earlier today and awaits a formal response from Bradictorian authorities. The Kingdom remains committed to safeguarding its sovereignty and upholding regional security.
