Government of Arcadia — Official News Release

Bradictoria Minister Admits Cyber Espionage

May 20, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

In a recent public statement, Lennox Steeves, the Minister of Online Security for the Empire of Bradictoria, openly admitted to engaging in cyber espionage activities against foreign governments.

Steeves candidly stated that he "was pretty damn good at it," revealing a level of pride in his hacking capabilities. This admission has sparked significant controversy and raised tensions between Bradictoria and its neighboring nations, including the Kingdom of Arcadia.

Officials from Arcadia have condemned the statement and reiterated their commitment to cybersecurity and the protection of their digital sovereignty.
