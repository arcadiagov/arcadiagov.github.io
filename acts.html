Arcadian Flag Act of 2025

Proclaimed on the Nineteenth Day of April, in the Year of Union Two Thousand and Twenty-Five Let it be known to all citizens, provinces, and representatives of the Kingdom of Arcadia, that the national flag of Arcadia, as depicted above, is hereby established as the sole and sovereign standard of the realm. The flag, bearing a noble cross argent upon a field of purple, with five crimson stars representing the unity and eternal vigilance of the realm, shall stand as the highest symbol of Arcadian identity, honor, and loyalty. It shall fly above all state buildings, diplomatic missions, ships of the realm, and military standards, and shall be displayed at all official ceremonies of the Crown and government. Any desecration, defacement, unauthorized alteration, or misuse of the Arcadian flag shall be deemed an act of disrespect against the Kingdom and its people. Such offenses may, depending on the nature and intent, constitute treason or high treason, and shall be punishable in accordance with the laws of the realm. The High Council shall be charged with ensuring the dignity of the national flag is upheld, and shall issue directives concerning its proper display, usage, and custody throughout the kingdom and abroad. This Act shall take full and immediate effect and be recorded among the laws of the Kingdom of Arcadia for perpetual observance.

Arcadian Heraldry Act of 2025

Proclaimed on the Nineteenth Day of April, in the Year of Union Two Thousand and Twenty-Five Let it be proclaimed, for all noble houses, cities, and loyal citizens of the Kingdom of Arcadia, that the sacred traditions of heraldry, armory, and symbolic regalia are henceforth formally regulated and enshrined in law by royal statute. The symbols of Arcadia—its coats of arms, blazons, banners, sigils, and ceremonial crests—are solemn representations of lineage, dignity, and allegiance to the realm and the Crown. As such, these symbols shall fall under the watch and governance of the High Council, who shall oversee their use, authorization, and lawful bearing in accordance with the honor of the Kingdom. No individual, house, guild, or institution shall bear or adopt heraldic insignia without warrant from the Crown or recognition by the High Council. The unauthorized use, falsification, or misappropriation of heraldic symbols shall be considered an offense against the dignity and sovereignty of the realm. In cases of significant abuse, such an act may constitute treason or high treason, and may be punished accordingly— including banishment, forfeiture, imprisonment, or death, as justice demands. The Royal Emblem of Arcadia, as depicted above, shall remain the sovereign symbol of the kingdom, representing its unity, legacy, and the enduring authority of its monarchy. Its use is strictly protected and reserved to those offices and persons granted permission by the High King or the High Council. Let this Act take full and immediate effect throughout all provinces and dominions of the Kingdom of Arcadia.

Arcadia Royal Blade Act of 2025

Let it be declared by royal statute, under the seal and authority of His Lordship, the Left-Honorable Lord High King of Arcadia, that the blade known by ancient name as Sting, presently held in the royal armory and borne at state occasion by the High King’s own hand, is henceforth recognized as an object of sovereign distinction and national heritage. The sword Sting is hereby established as Royal Property of the Crown of Arcadia, inseparable from the dignity and authority of the High King’s Office. Possession of the blade shall not, in itself, confer the title or privileges of the High King, nor imply lawful succession thereto. Rather, it shall serve as a symbolic extension of the royal office, its bearer acting only with such authority as has been granted by law, custom, or royal command. The presence of Sting shall signify the Crown’s enduring legacy and the sovereign unity of Arcadia, regardless of who bears it. Be it further enacted that any act of theft, unauthorized handling, concealment, damage, defilement, or destruction of the sword Sting shall be deemed a high act of treason against the Crown. Such an offense shall be tried under the laws of royal justice and punished to the fullest extent permitted by Arcadian and Imperial law. This Act shalll take full effect as of this Nineteenth Day of April, in the Year of Our Sovereign Lord 2025.

Arcadian Treason Act of 2025

Proclaimed on the Nineteenth Day of April, in the Year of Union Two Thousand and Twenty-Five This Act is established for the recognition, definition, and punishment of treason in all its forms, as committed within the lands and jurisdictions of the Kingdom of Arcadia. Three degrees of treason shall be recognized by the Crown: treason, high treason, and imperial treason. Treason shall encompass acts of disloyalty or betrayal against the governance of Arcadia, including sedition, espionage, unlawful rebellion, or willful obstruction of royal authority. High treason shall mean any crime directly against the Crown or the person and dignity of the Lord High King, such as attempted regicide, usurpation, or leading insurrection. Imperial treason, distinct in kind, refers to offenses that endanger the unity or integrity of the Eternal Union and the standing of Arcadia within it; such cases shall be referred to the Emperor and the Imperial Court for determination and judgment. The High King and the Emperor may each, within their own domains, declare acts to be treasonous, and such definitions shall hold the force of law. Punishments for treason may include imprisonment, banishment, seizure of property, fines of grave weight, or death, in accordance with the severity of the act and judgment passed. High treason shall bear the heaviest penalties under Arcadian law, while imperial treason, once declared, shall fall under the direct jurisdiction of the Empire. All penalties may be mitigated or pardoned only by the Crown or, where appropriate, by imperial grace. Let this Act be recorded and upheld throughout all territories and dominions of Arcadia, and be made known to all subjects, officers, and courts as a matter of solemn law. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its proclamation.
