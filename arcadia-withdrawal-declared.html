Arcadia Declares Withdrawal Countdown from Treaty of Eternal Union

April 17, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

The Lord High King of Arcadia has formally declared the beginning of the withdrawal countdown from the Treaty of Eternal Union with the Great Gheetan Empire. As per Article V, Section 3 of the treaty, no party may exit within the first six months following ratification. As of today, that countdown has officially begun.

Arcadia will gain legal eligibility to initiate withdrawal on October 17, 2025. Until then, the Kingdom remains bound by all obligations of the treaty.
