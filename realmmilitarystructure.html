The Arcadian Realm Military Structure

Apply to the Armed Forces

The military forces of the Kingdom Realm of Arcadia exist to uphold sovereignty, defend territory, and advance the national interest under the supreme authority of The Lord High King and the High Council.

Branches of the Arcadian Armed Forces

  • Royal Arcadian Army (RAA): Ground defense, land warfare, border security.
  • Arcadian Naval Fleet (ANF): Sea-based operations, naval dominance, coastal protection.
  • Arcadian Air Wing (AAW): Aerial superiority, strategic air support, airspace integrity.
  • Arcadian Special Operations Command (ASOC): Intelligence, elite warfare, national crisis response.

Military Command Structure

  • Lord High King: Supreme Commander of all military branches.
  • Chief of Defense Staff: Highest military officer, central strategic coordinator.
  • Branch Commanders: Generals/Admirals responsible for specific service branches.
  • Regional Commanders: Zone-based oversight, local force coordination.
