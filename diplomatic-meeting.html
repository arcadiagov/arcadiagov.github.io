Official Announcement

This afternoon, a distinguished summit will convene in the capital of the Great Gheetan Empire, bringing together the sovereign leaders of the Kingdom of Arcadia, the Great Gheetan Empire, and the territory of Bradictoria.

Discussions will focus on strengthening diplomatic ties, security collaboration, and advancing shared interests across the region.

The Kingdom of Arcadia reaffirms its commitment to peaceful engagement and the pursuit of lasting stability through these diplomatic efforts.
