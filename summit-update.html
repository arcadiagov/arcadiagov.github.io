Update: Intermicronational Summit Scheduled for May 24

May 23, 2025 – Capital City, Arcadia

The Kingdom Realm of Arcadia reaffirms its participation in the upcoming intermicronational summit, set to take place on Saturday, May 24, 2025, at 2:00 PM. His Lordship, the Lord High King of Arcadia, will represent the Kingdom Realm during this high-stakes diplomatic meeting.

Leaders attending include Emperor Bradley Christie of Bradictoria, Emperor Holden Porter of the Great Gheetan Empire, and His Lordship. The summit will focus on defense alliances, trade strategy, and frameworks for intermicronational cooperation.

With growing tensions and shifting allegiances across the micronational sphere, this summit is expected to play a pivotal role in reshaping diplomatic norms and forging new strategic alignments.

Security arrangements are under final review and are being coordinated by all participating states. Citizens are advised to follow official Arcadian communications for real-time updates.
