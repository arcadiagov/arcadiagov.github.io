Arcadia Joins the Great Gheetan Empire

Date: April 20, 2025

In a historic move, the Kingdom of Arcadia has officially joined the Great Gheetan Empire under the Treaty of Eternal Union. The treaty, which was ratified by both parties earlier this month, ensures mutual cooperation, cultural exchange, and a unified stance on defense and economic development.

High King Blakney declared this moment "a new dawn for Arcadia," emphasizing the benefits of a strong alliance with the Empire. Citizens can expect changes in intercolonial governance, streamlined trade agreements, and increased infrastructural investment throughout the realm.

New Acts Proposed by the Government

Date: April 18, 2025

Following Arcadia’s accession to the Gheetan Empire, the High Council has begun drafting a series of legislative acts aimed at aligning Arcadian policy with imperial standards. These acts will address trade regulation, intercolonial transportation, and civil rights protections under the new union.

The legislative process is expected to include a public commentary period, ensuring transparency and civic participation in shaping the new legal framework.

Arcadian Cultural Festival Announced

Date: March 30, 2025

The Ministry of Culture has announced a week-long cultural festival to celebrate Arcadian heritage and its new relationship with the Great Gheetan Empire. Events will include traditional music, cuisine, art exhibitions, and forums on the future of Arcadian identity in a broader imperial context.

Royal Archives Digitization Project Underway

Date: March 10, 2025

The Royal Archivist’s office has launched an ambitious digitization initiative to preserve Arcadia’s historical records. The project will ensure that key documents—including the Constitution and foundational charters—are accessible to all citizens through the government website.
