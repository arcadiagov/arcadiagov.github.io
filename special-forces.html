Special Forces & Elite Units

Arcadian Special Operations Command (ASOC)

Role:

ASOC is the elite rapid response and covert operations unit of the Arcadian Armed Forces, specializing in counterterrorism, reconnaissance, and high-risk missions.

  • Direct action and sabotage
  • Hostage rescue and extraction
  • Special reconnaissance and intelligence gathering

Royal Guard

Role:

The Royal Guard protects the Lord High King, the royal family, and key government installations. They maintain highest security standards and ceremonial duties.

  • Close protection and VIP security
  • Ceremonial guard duties
  • Counter-assault and rapid intervention

Arcadian Rangers

Role:

Highly trained for operations in difficult terrain, including forests and mountains, the Rangers excel in guerrilla warfare and long-range reconnaissance.

  • Wilderness survival and tracking
  • Ambush and hit-and-run tactics
  • Forward observation and target designation
