About the Kingdom of Arcadia

The Kingdom of Arcadia, founded in 2019, is a sovereign micronation ruled by a High King and a structured High Council. Our nation prides itself on its rich history, strong government, and dedication to the prosperity of its citizens. Learn more about our governance, culture, and national policies as you explore our website.

Our Governance

Arcadia is a monarchy with a detailed constitution. The High King holds executive and legislative power, assisted by the High Council in matters of state. The High Council oversees governance and the nation's legal system, ensuring the stability and prosperity of our great kingdom.
